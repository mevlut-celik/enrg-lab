Our Group

Our research group was founded by Dr. Emrah Tiras in October 2020, right after he joined the Physics Department at Erciyes University as an Asst. Professor. Our group is an active member of the Fermilab and CERN experiments, and we run an active research program in experimental high-energy physics, neutrino physics, particle detectors, and accelerator technologies. .
